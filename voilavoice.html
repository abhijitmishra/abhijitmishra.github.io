What is Voila Voice

Voila Voice is an AI platform built to make knowledge easier to share across languages, dialects, and formats. In plain terms, it helps turn slides, documents, and web content into something people can actually consume and reuse across a wider range of audiences.

The core idea is multilingual dialect-oriented translations, not just word-for-word conversion. The platform combines video generation, voice and avatar-based delivery, interactive quizzes, and AI-guided explanation so technical or educational content can travel more naturally across regions, learner profiles, and communication styles.

Mission

  • Make content reachable to a global audience.
  • Reduce language barriers in learning, teaching, and communication.
  • Reduce modal barriers by converting static documents into video, voice, and interactive formats.
  • Preserve meaning while improving accessibility, localization, and usability.

Core Platform Themes

  • Multilingual dialect-oriented translations for presentations, documents, and links.
  • AI-generated video lessons with avatars, voice options, and editable outputs.
  • Interactive quizzes and guided explanation through the AL learning companion.
  • Flexible delivery modes for educators, students, professionals, creators, and enterprise teams.

Role

I serve as Co-founder and CTO at Voila Voice. For me, this is a direct product extension of my work in NLP, speech, multimodal AI, and human-centered systems.

  • Help shape the technical direction of the platform and its AI capabilities.
  • Translate research ideas in language, voice, and multimodal intelligence into usable product features.
  • Focus on systems that improve access, explanation, and communication across languages, dialects, and formats.
  • Support product thinking around how TiLT can make educational and professional content more widely usable.
