VoilaVoice

A concise overview of the platform mission, core product, and role in building it.

Summary

VoilaVoice is an AI platform focused on making content reachable to a global audience by reducing language and modal barriers. The platform turns presentations, documents, and web content into multilingual video experiences with translation, localization, quizzes, AI-guided explanation, and exportable formats for teaching, learning, and professional communication.

Its product flow emphasizes one-click conversion of static material into accessible, shareable content across 30+ languages, with support for voice, text, visuals, avatars, and interactive learning modes.

Mission

  • Make content reachable to a global audience.
  • Reduce language barriers in learning, teaching, and communication.
  • Reduce modal barriers by converting static documents into video, voice, and interactive formats.
  • Preserve meaning while improving accessibility, localization, and usability.

Core Platform Themes

  • Multilingual translation and localization for presentations, documents, and links.
  • AI-generated video lessons with avatars, voice options, and editable outputs.
  • Interactive quizzes and guided explanation through the AL learning companion.
  • Support for educators, students, professionals, creators, and enterprise teams.

Role

I serve as Co-founder and CTO at VoilaVoice. The work extends my research background in NLP, speech, multimodal AI, and human-centered systems into a practical platform for multilingual knowledge access and communication.

  • Help shape the technical direction of the platform and its AI capabilities.
  • Translate research ideas in language, voice, and multimodal intelligence into product features.
  • Focus on systems that improve access, explanation, and communication across languages and formats.
