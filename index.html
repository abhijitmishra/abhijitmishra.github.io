About

I am an AI researcher and educator working at the intersection of language, vision, speech, and neuro-signals. Over the past 10+ years, I have worked on building and scaling foundational models, generative systems, and multimodal and multilingual AI systems across research and product settings.

My research integrates cognitive science with modern machine learning to build robust, human-centered, and practically deployable systems for healthcare, assistive intelligence, and advanced language technologies.

I hold a PhD in Computer Science and Engineering from IIT Bombay, with specialization in machine learning and natural language processing.

My industry journey includes high-impact roles at Apple Siri and IBM Research. In addition to my academic role, I serve as Co-founder and CTO at VoilaVoice, supporting practical transfer of voice AI research in an intentionally low-profile capacity.
